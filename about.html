About Us

    

    
 About Us GreenRoost Poultry

Founded in 2020, GreenRoost Poultry was born out of a passion for healthy living and sustainable farming. From humble beginnings in the heart of Ghana, we’ve grown into a trusted name for premium poultry products that nourish families and support local businesses.

At GreenRoost, we specialize in ethically-raised broilers, high-yield layers, and 100% organic poultry feed. Our chickens are raised with care, free from harmful additives, and our eggs are gathered fresh daily, ensuring quality and taste you can trust.

We’re more than just a farm — we’re a community committed to healthier food systems, responsible farming practices, and building a stronger future for agriculture in Ghana.
🎯 Our Mission

To provide fresh, ethically-raised poultry products that promote healthy living and uplift local communities through sustainable agriculture.
👁️ Our Vision

To become Ghana’s leading name in responsible poultry farming, known for our quality, transparency, and community-driven values.
📈 Our Journey
Year	Milestone
